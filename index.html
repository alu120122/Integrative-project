.01 About the Product

We have developed a meat wich can provide us with the vitamins and minerals found in animal meat that are vital for health, such as vitamin B12 and Iron that are normally only obtained from animal meat.

In addition, ECOMEAT has the goal of making this imitation meat accessible to everyone but also rich in quality for those who purchase it.

For this reason, it contributes to the UN objective number 2 "Zero hunger" and number 3 "Good health and well-being".

.02 Why this product?

Our product was made with the purpose of achieving one of the sustainable development goals, so that groups of people with limited resources have access to cheaper food, but just as nutritious as high-priced one. Thanks to the ingredients that make it up, our product is accessible and provides all necessary nutrients to remain healthy.

On the other hand, our logo arose from the desire to represent the animals that are saved thanks to the composition of the same product and to demostrate that the fact that a food is nutritious does not necessarily mean that it is of animal origin.

Finally, our meat has the texture of common meat like chicken, plus it can be used to create new dishes.

ECOMEAT is a food which, when consumed, contributes in the same way or even better than meat, since it provides the same nutrients but, in addition, it avoids cholesterol and other bad effects of the consumption of animal meat.
